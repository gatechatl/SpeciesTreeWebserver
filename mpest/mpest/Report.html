Introduction

Here we present a gene tree report comparing the gene tree against the species tree. RF distance is calculated between the gene tree and the species tree by removing the missing taxa from the species tree. Triple distance is also calculated. The pdf file from the "Pairwise Comparison" column compares the species tree and gene tree, species tree is on the left and gene tree is on the right. In addition on the "GeneTree Diff Highlight" column, we present gene trees with conflicting branches (compared to species tree) highlighted with blue. Gray highlight indicate branches that are the same.
